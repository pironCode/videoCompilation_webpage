All you need to know about forex

Forex, short for foreign exchange, is the global marketplace where currencies are traded. It is the largest and most liquid financial market in the world, with trillions of dollars exchanged daily. Forex trading involves buying one currency while simultaneously selling another, and it operates 24 hours a day, five days a week.
